How It Works

The entire process is faster than ordering a pizza.

Sign Up

Sign up on your school page and pay the deposit. You can ask us any questions, and one of our team members will be personally assigned to your case to ensure you are as delighted as all of our previous customers.

We Bring Your Moving Supplies

We deliver your boxes and tape (if you request them) before your pickup date. You can spend more time studying and less time stressing!

We Pick Everything Up

Pack all your items into boxes and sit back and relax as we pick them up from your doorstep! You will only be billed for what we pick up.

We Bring Everything Back

While, you've been crushing your summer internship, travelling abroad, or doing whatever you chose to do over the summer, you can rest assured knowing your possessions are safe and secure. When it comes time to return to campus, we will deliver your items right to your doorstep.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't see your question here, fill out our contact form, and we will get back to you ASAP!

  • What do I have to do once I place an order?

    Just sit back and relax, and we will deliver your boxes (as many as you request) and tape two weeks before pickup day! During finals week, we will come to your doorstep and take your boxes and other items away!

  • When can my items be delivered in fall?

    We offer two to three delivery dates for fall that are conveniently scheduled before the first of classes at your university.

  • Do I need to know how many boxes and other items I will have prior to pickup?

    Nope! We know how hard it is to predict how much stuff how will have in advance. For that reason, you can simply sign up, and we will take whatever amount of items you have on pickup day, whether that be 1 or 100.

  • Do you serve students living off campus?

    Absolutely! We will serve any student, including those living off campus.

  • What does the list price cover? Are there any extra charges for dropoff or pickup?

    The list prices covers everything, from pickup to storage to dropoff. There are no extra charges for any of these services. The only additional charge is a box/tape charge if you would like that delivered to you two weeks before pickup day.

  • How will I be charged?

    An $80 deposit is due at the time of your registration.

  • What is your privacy policy?

    We will never provide your information to any third party. It will remain strictly confidential. Furthermore, all credit card transactions are completed through a secure payment system and we as the vendor will never see your payment information.
